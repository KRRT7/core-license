### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

#### Does using this software via an API trigger the AGPLv3 copyleft?

Yes. The AGPLv3 contains a network-communication clause. If your proprietary software interacts with this program over a local or remote network interface, you must open-source your entire application stack unless you purchase a valid commercial waiver (Option 2 or Option 3).

#### What happens if our company size or region changes mid-year?

License compliance is evaluated on a rolling basis at the time of your renewal. If your corporate metrics scale up or down, simply adjust your funding target tier on your next regular billing cycle.

#### Are these contributions tax-deductible?

Direct donations to the PSF (Option 3) go directly to a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and may qualify for deductions depending on your country. Contribution Pool allocations (Option 2) split your funding targets across engineering and non-profit services; please consult your corporate tax professional.
