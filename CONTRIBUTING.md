# Contributing

This project accepts contributions only if the project can keep offering the same licensing choices to downstream users: AGPLv3 by default, plus separately signed evaluation licenses, commercial licenses, and philanthropy waivers.

## Inbound Contribution License

By submitting a contribution, you represent that you have the right to submit it and you license that contribution to the project maintainer under:

1. GNU Affero General Public License version 3 only; and
2. the project's separately documented evaluation license, commercial license, and philanthropy waiver paths.

This means the project maintainer may include your contribution in AGPLv3 releases and may also grant evaluation or proprietary-use rights for the contribution through signed evaluation licenses, commercial licenses, or philanthropy waiver certificates.

## Your Responsibilities

Do not submit code, text, assets, data, or other materials unless you have the right to license them this way.

If your employer, client, school, or another organization may own your work, confirm that you are allowed to contribute before submitting.

Do not submit third-party materials unless their license is compatible with this project and you clearly identify the source and license.

## Developer Certificate

Projects may also require signed commits using the Developer Certificate of Origin:

```text
Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
```

If a project requires sign-off, every commit in a contribution must include it.
